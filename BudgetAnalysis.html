Groundwater budget calculations based on CVHM

A basic assumption of the NPSAT approach is that the flow is steady state. To derive a representative steady state solution it is important that the storage change is approximately 0.
The following plot below shows the monthly and yearly cumulative storage change based on the CVHM model.
Ovarall there is a significant decline in storage, however there are several periods where the storage change is very close to 0.
The longest recent period spans 21 years from 12/1977 to 4/1999, where the storage has been declined 35.5 mACFT with respect to initial conditions.
However, the most recent period spans 11 years from 12/1991 to 9/2003.
